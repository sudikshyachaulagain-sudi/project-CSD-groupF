Nepali Festivals

Nepali festivals are vibrant celebrations that showcase the country’s rich cultural heritage. From the grandeur of Dashain to the joy of Tihar, each festival brings communities together in tradition and festivity. These celebrations are marked by unique rituals, delicious foods, and colorful attire that reflect Nepal’s diverse identity.

Dashain

Largest Nepali festival

Family gatherings, blessings, and celebratory rituals for good over evil.

Tihar

Festival of lights

Homes glow with lamps, music fills the streets, and prosperity is celebrated.

Holi

Festival of colors

Spring arrives with vibrant powders, music, and joyful gatherings.

Maghi

Tharu community festival

Traditional foods, cultural dances, and community celebration.
