About Nepal

Nepal is a beautiful country rich in culture, traditions, festivals, and natural beauty. It is home to many ethnic groups, each with unique customs, languages, and lifestyles. From Mount Everest to peaceful temples and heritage sites, Nepal offers a perfect blend of natural and cultural attractions.
